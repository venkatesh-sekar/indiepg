- [x] (1) Confirm CSRF protection on every state-changing endpoint (or that the auth model makes it moot) — done 2026-06-24: the CSRF gate lives in `requireAuth` (cookie + unsafe method needs same-origin Origin/Referer or `X-Indiepg-Csrf`, else 409 CodeSafety before the handler).
